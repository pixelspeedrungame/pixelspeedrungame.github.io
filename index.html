How fast can you lead your pixel to victory? Pixel Speedrun is an ultimate platformer game, where you face an incredible trial – to pass all the levels as quickly as you can, without committing any confusions. Every misstep keeps you from the desired score, so keep your reflexes sharp and glide across the terrains!

How to Play Pixel Speedrun

This game is available online on Construct, which allows you to keep track of your achievements and maintain your place in the ratings. Utilise all kinds of tricks and shortcuts, while enjoying the competitive spirit of the game!

The mechanics themselves are pretty easy, as your little cube can just run or jump. Apply these skills to diverse surfaces and watch your pixel practically fly over the barriers, bypassing dangerous spots!

Select Your Mode

Unblocked 76 version of the game also includes several regimes with different aspects and peculiarities:

  • Classic
  • Practice
  • Collect
  • Challenges
  • Blind
  • Chaos

Depending on your aim, you may switch between these variants, checking out multiple trials and pushing your limits to the edge. Whether you just want to perfect your skills, or you wish to venture for a more sophisticated route, Pixel Speedrun Unblocked has a lot to offer.
